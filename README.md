## 🌍 About Us
The **Society of Marine Engineers Kenya (SOMEK)** is the national professional body representing marine engineers, naval architects, offshore specialists, and maritime technology practitioners. 

Founded to bridge the gap between maritime operational expertise and formal engineering regulation, SOMEK works in consultation with regulatory bodies like the **Engineers Board of Kenya (EBK)** and international maritime frameworks (IMO conventions) to build robust local capacity, advocate for statutory recognition, and drive technical innovation in the region.

- 🌐 **Official Web Portal:** [somekenya.org](https://somekenya.org) *(Secondary alias: `ksome.org`)*
- 📜 **Institutional Status:** Professional Society registered under the Societies Act (Kenya)
- 📧 **General Inquiries:** [info@somekenya.org](mailto:partnerships@somekenya.org)

## 🤝 How to Get Involved

Whether you are a practicing marine engineer, an offshore researcher, or a FOSS developer, we welcome contributions across our technical and regulatory working groups:

1. **Regulatory & Technical Collaboration:** Review and contribute to draft white papers, LaTeX document templates, and policy submissions via Pull Requests.
2. **Open Data & Maritime Tooling:** Help build local maritime calculations, open datasets, and web utilities tagged `help-wanted`.
3. **Professional Membership:** Learn about registration grades, student chapters, and continuous professional development (CPD) at [somekenya.org/membership](https://somekenya.org/membership).
